Recipe

Chocolate cake

"Here is a fast and easy recipe for a classic desert. Make it with joy, share it with friends and family and I hope you enjoy it!"

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total Time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

Dark choclate: 200 gr

Sugar: 150 gr

Butter: 200 gr

Eggs: 4x

Instructions

1

First, break the eggs and mix them well.

2

Add melted butter.

3

Add everything and mix.

4

Bake in the oven.
